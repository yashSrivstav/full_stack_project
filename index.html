I used to ponder over the fact about "Why guy bestfriends are said to be a bliss in every girl's life", but I never knew that I would end up writing a letter for a boy like you!

It all started when you smiled and uttered a formal "hi" and my mind whispered "Just another guy". You gradually shifted from those formal "hello-s" to "how do you do" , and I started to reply with keen interest "I want a treat too" ! As we began to open up, we started doing every reckless things that I had only seen in movies. And since then, life has changed a lil bit for me.

Strolling on my terrace while listening to Willie Nelson's song "I'd trade all of my tomorrows for just one yesterday", made me feel nostalgic about the days before this pandemic Covid 19 devastated each one of us.

Only if there was a way I could go back to the early month of January where the cold winds touched my face and there was no pandemic news flashing on the TV. The days where I felt carefree even after coming home exhausted and frustrated. I miss the days where I could laugh my heart loud with my friends while they went insane because now, all I have is some stupid video calls or zoom meetings.
